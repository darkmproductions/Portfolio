About Me:

Hello, my name is Noah, and I am a 3d character animator. For six years I’ve been teaching myself the software Blender 3D and have been constantly trying to find ways to better my craft as an animator and artist. I started getting into Blender during the beginning of Covid, and from then on out I knew I wanted to go somewhere within the 3D art field. As time went on, I realized I had a passion for animation, and from 2024 onward I have been developing my skills as a character animator. My determination to become better at animation has driven me to share my works online so that I can not only share what I have done, but get feedback to grow further. Now along with my own work, I am looking for work as a freelancer, ready to help hoping to help anyone who's work might benefit from an animated touch.
